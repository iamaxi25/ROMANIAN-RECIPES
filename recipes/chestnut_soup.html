Chestnut Soup

Description

Preparation Times

  • Preparation Time: 15 min
  • Cooking Time: 30 min
  • Ready in: 45 min

Ingredients

  • 250g chestnuts
  • 1 onion
  • 1 leek
  • 1 clove of garlic
  • 1 glass of chicken soup or vegetable soup
  • 1 glass of water
  • 50ml cooking cream
  • oil
  • salt
  • pepper

STEPS

  1. Chestnuts are grown cross-wise so that they can cook faster.
  2. Boil water in a saucepan. When the water starts to boil, add the chestnuts. Let it boil for 15 minutes. After they are boiled, leave to cool.
  3. Finely chop the onion and leek. In another saucepan with a little oil, fry until soft. Add the chopped garlic and cook a little more.
  4. The chestnuts are peeled and their core is added over the vegetables in the pan. Saut√©.
  5. Add the soup and water. Season with salt and pepper. Let it cook for about 15 minutes.
  6. The contents of the saucepan are mashed with a blender until the consistency of a cream is obtained.
  7. You can add more lukewarm water if the chestnut soup is not liquid enough.
  8. Put the saucepan back on the heat and add the cooking cream and (optionally) thyme.
  9. Chestnut soup is served warm.
