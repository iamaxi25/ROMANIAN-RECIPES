Pumpkin cake

Description

Preparation Times

  • Preparation Time: 20 min
  • Cooking Time: 45 min
  • Ready in: 65 min

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 200g flour
  • 200g cooked pumpkin
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • a pinch of sodium bicarbonate
  • 80g of sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla essence
  • 80 ml of oil
  • 75 ml of milk
  • orange peel

STEPS

  1. The pumpkin used in this recipe must be prepared in advance, either boiled or baked. Mash the obtained pumpkin until a fine puree is obtained.
  2. Preheat the oven to 180 degrees Celsius. Cover the round tray or cake form with a baking sheet.
  3. In a bowl mix the eggs, vanilla essence, orange peel. Mix until a whitish composition is obtained that doubles in volume.
  4. Add the oil, milk and cold pumpkin puree. It homogenizes.
  5. Mix the flour, baking powder and baking soda separately. This mixture is gradually added over the egg mixture. The resulting dough should have the consistency of a slightly thicker pancake batter.
  6. The cake dough is poured into the form and baked for 40-45 minutes at 180 degrees Celsius or until it passes the toothpick test.
  7. The pumpkin cake is served after it has cooled.
