Sarmale

Description

Time of preparation

  • 45 min

Ingredients

  • 1-2 pickled cabbage
  • 1kg minced pork
  • 1 cup of rice(4-5 tablespoons
  • 1-2 medium onions
  • 200g tomato paste
  • salt,pepper,thyme,bay leaf,paprika,peppercorns
  • 2-3 tablespoons of oil

Steps

  1. Clean the cabbage from the outer leaves and spines and choose the sheets in which the meat will be wrapped.
  2. Clean and wash the onion, then finely chop it. Put it in a saucepan with oil and a little water and leave it until it turns golden.
  3. Mix the fried and cooled onion with the meat, pepper, salt and rice, which was also boiled and cooled, in a bowl.
  4. Take the composition the size of a walnut and put it on a cabbage leaf, which is rolled with the edges inside.
  5. Stop the smaller cabbage leaves and chop the scales to form a layer for the sarmales.
  6. In a tall saucepan, place the sarmales and ribs on top of the chopped cabbage, and another layer of chopped cabbage on top. Pour the water-soaked broth over them, put the bay leaves and a few peppercorns. Leave to boil, on low heat, for 2 hours. From time to time, add water to prevent sticking.
